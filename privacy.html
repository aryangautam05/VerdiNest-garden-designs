Privacy Policy

Committed to protecting your privacy and data security with transparency and integrity.

Introduction

At VerdiNest Garden Designs, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services. Please read this policy carefully to understand our views and practices regarding your personal data.

By accessing or using our website, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

Personal Information You Provide

We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, mailing address, phone number, and any other information you provide when contacting us or subscribing to our services. This information enables us to provide personalized service and communications that serve your landscaping and garden design needs.

Automatically Collected Information

As you navigate our website, we automatically collect technical data including your IP address, browser type and version, time zone, and usage patterns. This data helps us understand user behavior, detect potential security threats, and improve site performance.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We utilize cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your user experience, remember your preferences, provide personalized content, and analyze site traffic. Cookies may be first-party or third-party and you can choose to disable them, though some website features may be limited as a result.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, and maintain our website, including personalizing your experience with tailored content and services.
  • To communicate effectively with you, respond to inquiries, and deliver customer support regarding your garden design projects or services.
  • To send newsletters and marketing materials that reflect your interests and preferences, keeping you informed about new services, offers, and updates from VerdiNest.
  • To conduct data analytics, understand user interactions, and continually improve our website, service offerings, and digital experience.
  • To detect and prevent fraudulent activities, unauthorized access, or security breaches to protect you and our systems.
  • To fulfill legal obligations and enforce our terms, ensure compliance, and handle any disputes or claims.

Sharing Your Information

VerdiNest does not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We share your data only with trusted service providers assisting us in site operation, marketing, payment processing, or delivery of services, bound by confidentiality agreements.

We may disclose your information when required by law, regulatory authorities, or in response to legal processes such as subpoenas or court orders. Additionally, your information may be disclosed in connection with mergers, acquisitions, or asset sales as part of company transitions.

Data Security

Protecting your personal information is paramount. We implement industry-standard security controls including encryption, firewalls, intrusion detection, and strict access management to safeguard your data from unauthorized access or disclosure.

While we strive to maintain highest security standards, no internet transmission or electronic storage is completely secure. We urge you to also adopt security measures on your devices and networks.

Your Data Protection Rights

You have rights under applicable data protection laws, including but not limited to:

  • The right to access your personal data and receive a copy of what we hold about you upon request.
  • The right to request correction or updating of inaccurate or incomplete data to ensure accuracy.
  • The right to request deletion of your personal data in certain situations, often known as the "right to be forgotten".
  • The right to restrict or object to processing your personal data for marketing or other legitimate interests.
  • The right to data portability to obtain and reuse your data across different services.
  • The right to withdraw your consent where processing is based on explicit consent.

You may exercise these rights by contacting us at privacy@verdinnest.com. We will respond timely and in accordance with regulations.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by websites to remember your preferences and aid in site functionality. We use a variety of cookies including:

  • Essential cookies: Necessary for basic website functions and security.
  • Performance cookies: Collect anonymous data to improve website performance.
  • Functional cookies: Remember choices like language or region to personalize experience.
  • Advertising cookies: Track your browsing habits to provide targeted advertisements across platforms.

You can control cookie settings via your browser or device preferences, and some browsers offer opt-out mechanisms for targeted advertising.

Third-Party Links and Services

Our website may contain links to third-party websites and services. These are for your convenience only and do not imply our endorsement. We have no control over their privacy policies and encourage you to read their policies before providing any personal information.

Children's Privacy

VerdiNest does not knowingly collect personal data from children under 16 years old. If we become aware that we have unintentionally gathered data from a minor, we will promptly delete it. Our services and website are designed for adult users and landscaping professionals.

Updates to This Privacy Policy
